Fraud Awareness: Stay Smart, Stay Safe

Fraud and scams are on the rise -- and they’re getting smarter.  As your credit union we’re here to help you stay one step ahead. Here’s what you need to know:

 Phone Scams (“Vishing”)  - Fraudsters may call you pretending to be from your credit union or a tech support company, asking for your account number, PIN, or password.

If you think you’ve been a victim of fraud or suspect suspicious activity, contact us immediately at 999-999-9999 or visit your local branch. Our fraud prevention team is ready to assist you.
